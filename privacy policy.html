Protecting Your Privacy

Vintage Summer is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers and site visitors. If you have any questions, drop us a line at hello@VintageSummer.com.



Sharing Your Information

We do not sell any of your personal data. However, we share your data with:

  • Companies in the Vintage Summer group.
  • Payment service providers, warehouses, order packers, and delivery companies.
  • Professional service providers such as marketing agencies and website hosts.
  • Credit reference agencies, law enforcement, and fraud prevention agencies.
  • Companies approved by you, like social media sites.


Marketing Messages

If you’ve opted in, we’ll send you marketing messages. You can stop receiving these at any time by:

  • Clicking on the ‘unsubscribe’ link in any email.
  • Contacting our Customer Care team.


Keeping Your Information

We’ll only hold onto your information as long as you are a customer. If you haven’t logged on for three years, we will delete your information. You can also request deletion at any time.



Your Rights

  • The right to be informed about how your personal information is used.
  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • The right to request deletion of your data in some circumstances.
  • The right to stop direct marketing messages.
  • The right to request transfer of elements of your data.
  • The right to complain to your data protection regulator.


Changes to How We Protect Your Privacy

We may change this page from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated clearly on our website or via email.
