When you shop with us or access our services and websites (our “Websites”), these terms apply. They outline our expectations from each other and provide helpful information. If you need assistance, please contact our Customer Care team.

We regularly update these terms and our help pages, so please check back before you shop to ensure you are aware of the latest updates.



About You


  • You must be at least 16 years old;
  • You must have a credit or debit card that we accept;
  • You must be authorized to use that credit or debit card.

Some products sold through our Websites may not be suitable for individuals under 18 years old.



Placing an Order


We appoint Webxpay at Green Lanka Tower, 5th Floor, 46/46, Navam Mawatha, Sri Lanka, as our billing agent to invoice and collect payment for your purchases.

Upon placing an order, you will receive an acknowledgment email confirming receipt. We perform a pre-authorization check to ensure sufficient funds are available on your card.

Your order is accepted only once payment has been approved and debited from your card. You may cancel your order within a short period after ordering; however, changes cannot be made once the order is placed.



Prices and Product Descriptions


Occasionally, errors may occur in pricing or product descriptions. If we discover an error with your order, we will inform you promptly and offer the option to confirm at the correct price or cancel the order.

Prices include VAT where applicable. Additional delivery charges may apply based on your order value and chosen delivery method.



Delivery


You will be presented with various delivery options before finalizing your order. While we strive to meet delivery times, delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances.



Returns and Refunds


If you wish to return an item, it must be in its original condition. We accept returns for free within 4 days of delivery for a full refund via the original payment method.

Items returned after this period may incur additional shipping costs. Certain items such as underwear cannot be returned if hygiene seals are broken.



Faulty Goods


If you believe an item is faulty, please contact our Customer Care team immediately with details about the issue. Under Sri Lankan Consumer Law, you may be entitled to a replacement or refund for major failures.

Your privacy is important to us. Please review our Privacy Policy for details on how we handle your information.
